Who we are

We are an animal rescue orginization in the community.

We specialize in adoption, catch/release, spade/neuter, and vaccines.

What we believe

WE LOVE PETS believes that our community and city should

work together to care for the animals that are stray, feral, and domesticated.

It's our job to stop overpopulation, take in surrendered pets, find loving homes,

and give any medical attention for the animals in need.
